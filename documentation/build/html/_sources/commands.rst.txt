Available bot commands 
======================

Commands for organizator:
-------------------------

   1) /listspec - list of moderators
   
   2) /add - add new moderator
   
   3) /change - change moderator status
   
   4) /listreg - list of organizators, who are checking out participants at that moment
   
   5) /surname - search for participant by last name
   
   6) /info - announcment to all participants
   
   7) /write - writing to certain person
   
   8) /count - total number of people in database
   
   9) /roomnum - list of people, who live in certain room
   
   10) /regchage - ready/not ready to check out participants
   
 
Commands for participant:
-------------------------
 
   1) /start - staring conversation with bot 
   
   2) /leave - submit an application for leaving
